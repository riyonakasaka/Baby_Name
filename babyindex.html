The Rise of Girls' Names Ending in 'a'

Based on Social Security Data Since 1980

By Riyo Nakasaka

In the United States, the name Olivia has dominated as the most popular name for girls for five consecutive years. As of 2023, Emma claimed the second spot, followed by Amelia in fourth, Sophia in fifth, and then Mia, Isabella, and Ava rounding out the top rankings. Interestingly, names ending with 'a' continue to trend, with Luna securing the tenth position. This trend underscores the enduring popularity and evolving preferences in American baby names, reflecting both traditional choices and contemporary influences.
